# R&D in AI era

**AI-assisted discovery:** Models accelerate hypothesis generation, molecular screening, materials discovery and biological structure prediction.

**Generative design:** Engineering teams use AI to explore design spaces constrained by cost, weight, performance, manufacturability and sustainability.

**Simulation and digital twins:** R&D reduces physical prototyping by using virtual testing and synthetic data.

**Research copilots:** Scientists and engineers use GenAI to summarize literature, draft protocols, write code and document experiments.

**AI governance in R&D:** Issues include intellectual property ownership, reproducibility, hallucinated scientific claims, model transparency and data rights.
