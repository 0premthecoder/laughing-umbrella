# Table on Management vs Business Function

| **Basis**          | **Management Functions**                 | **Business Functions**                       |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Meaning**        | Activities managers perform              | Specialized departments/areas                |
| **Focus**          | Managing the organization                | Performing operational work                  |
| **Nature**         | Universal and process-oriented           | Specialized and task-oriented                |
| **Applicability**  | Exists in every department               | Different functions have different expertise |
| **Responsibility** | Primarily managers                       | Functional specialists and managers          |
| **Scope**          | Cross-functional                         | Department-specific                          |
| **Objective**      | Achieve organizational goals effectively | Execute specialized business activities      |
| **Main Question**  | "How do we manage?"                      | "What business activity is performed?"       |
| **Examples**       | Planning, organizing, controlling...     | Marketing, finance, HR, operations...        |
