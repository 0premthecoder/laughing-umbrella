# Roles and Activities in Operation

|**ROLES**|**KEY ACTIVITIES**|**STRATEGIC GOALS**|
|---|---|---|
|Chief Operating Officer (COO)|Production planning, scheduling & execution|Deliver products/services On Time In Full (OTIF)|
|VP Supply Chain / Procurement|Supply chain management & vendor relations|Minimize total cost of production and delivery|
|Plant / Factory Manager|Inventory optimization & warehouse management|Achieve zero-defect quality standards|
|Quality Assurance Manager|Quality control & continuous improvement (Kaizen)|Build agile, resilient supply chains|
|Inventory & Logistics Manager|Capacity planning & resource allocation|Maximize asset utilization and operational efficiency|
|Lean / Six Sigma Process Lead|Lean manufacturing & waste elimination (MUDA)|Create operations as a source of competitive advantage|
