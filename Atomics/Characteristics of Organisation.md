# Characteristics of Organisation

Every Organisation exists for a reason i.e to produce goods, deliver services, or achieve a mission

without purpose there is no organization

They are made up of people who work together, one person acting alone is not an organization

Rules, norms, culture, hierarchy,  or market coordinate individual effort into collective output

Effective organisation sense environmental change and adapt in terms of product, strategy, structures those do not adapt perishes
