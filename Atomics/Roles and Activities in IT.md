# Roles and Activities in IT

| **ROLES**                         | **KEY ACTIVITIES**                                  | **STRATEGIC GOALS**                                    |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Chief Information Officer (CIO)   | IS&T strategy and technology roadmap                | Enable business operations through reliable, secure IT |
| Chief Technology Officer (CTO)    | Infrastructure management (servers, cloud, network) | Drive competitive advantage through digital innovation |
| Chief Data Officer (CDO)          | Cybersecurity and data protection                   | Protect organizational data and intellectual property  |
| IT Infrastructure & Security Lead | Enterprise application management (ERP, CRM, HRMS)  | Reduce IT costs through cloud and automation           |
| Enterprise Architect              | Data analytics, BI and AI/ML platforms              | Build data-driven decision-making capability           |
| Digital Transformation Lead       | Digital product development and DevOps              | Accelerate digital transformation across all functions |
