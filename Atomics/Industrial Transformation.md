# Industrial Transformation


| **Industry**          | **Transformation**                                                        | **Management Significance**                                                       |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Textiles**          | Domestic putting-out system ⇨ centralised mill; cotton output ×50 by 1800 | Factory discipline, shift systems, child/female labour debates, first labour laws |
| **Coal Mining**       | Shallow pits ⇨ deep shaft mining via steam pumps; output tripled          | Mine safety management, early occupational health crises                          |
| **Iron & Steel**      | Charcoal furnaces ⇨ coke blast furnaces; wrought iron for construction    | Large-scale capital investment, integrated production sites                       |
| **Transportation**    | Turnpike roads ⇨ canals ⇨ railways; freight costs fell 80%                | Supply chain management, logistics optimisation emerge as concepts                |
| **Agriculture**       | Enclosure, crop rotation, mechanisation (seed drill, thresher)            | Surplus rural labour migrates to cities; urbanisation management challenge        |
| **Banking & Finance** | Country banks, bill discounting, joint-stock companies, insurance         | Capital markets, corporate governance, shareholder accountability begin           |
