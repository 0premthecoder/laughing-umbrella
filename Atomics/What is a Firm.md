# What is a Firm

A firm is an individual business organization that produce goods or services. It is the basic unit of economic activity

| Characteristics        | Explaination                              |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Legal enitity          | Registered organization                   |
| Decision-making entity | Makes Strategic and operational decisions |
| Resource ownership     | Uses labor, capital, technology           |
| Profit orientation     | Seeks Returns                             |
| Brand Identity         | Operates under unique Identity            |

e.g Apple, Google, Burger king
