# Roles and Activities in R&D

|**ROLES**|**KEY ACTIVITIES**|**STRATEGIC GOALS**|
|---|---|---|
|Chief Technology Officer (CTO)|Basic and applied research programmed|Create breakthrough technologies for future competitive advantage|
|Chief Science Officer / R&D Director|New product and technology development|Build a defensible IP portfolio|
|Innovation Manager / Head of Labs|IP filing and portfolio management|Reduce time-to-market for new products|
|Product Development Engineer|Technology scouting & competitive intelligence|Maintain technology leadership in core domains|
|Intellectual Property (IP) Manager|Prototype development and testing|Generate revenue from licensing and technology transfer|
|University & Startup Partnership Lead|University partnerships & startup ecosystem management|Build innovation culture across the organization|
