# Future of Business Function

**Marketing**: Hyper-personalization at scale - Al generates individual-level
campaigns for a billion+ customers. Zero-party data replaces third-party
cookies.

**Finance**: The real-time CFO - blockchain settlement, continuous capital
optimization, Al handling —800/0 of FP&A. ESG metrics go fully mainstream.

**Human Resources**: People intelligence - attrition predicted six months ahead, Al
coaches replace annual reviews, skills-based hiring replaces degree-based filters.

**Operations**: Autonomous supply chains - self-rerouting Al, digital twins, 3D printing for
on-demand local manufacturing.

**Information Technology**: Everywhere and invisible - edge, IoT and 5G make IT
pervasive; low-code/no-code democratizes tech. Every function is a tech function.

**R&D**: Al-accelerated - AlphaFold-style models compress 10-year research programs to 18 months. Innovation velocity is the new competitive metric.

**Business Strategy**: Continuous and adaptive - 5-year plans 90-day OKR cycles.
Real-time sensing replaces annual planning. VUCA demands perpetual strategy.


**The Underlying Shift:**
All seven functions become data-rich, Al-augmented and continuously adaptive.
The boundary between functions softens; the boundary between firm and
ecosystem softens further.
