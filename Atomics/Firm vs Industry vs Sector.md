# Firm vs Industry vs Sector

### Comparison of Firm, Industry, and Sector

| **Basis**     | **Firm**                     | **Industry**             | **Sector**                      |
| ------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------- |
| **Meaning**   | Single business organization | Group of similar firms   | Broad economic category         |
| **Level**     | Micro                        | Meso                     | Macro                           |
| **Focus**     | Organization                 | Competition              | Economy                         |
| **Unit**      | One company                  | Many companies           | Many industries                 |
| **Example 1** | Nike                         | Footwear Industry        | Consumer goods sector           |
| **Example 2** | Tesla                        | Automobile / EV Industry | Manufacturing/Automotive Sector |
| **Example 3** | TCS                          | IT Services Industry     | Services/Technology Sector      |
