# Frederick Winslow Taylor (Scientific Management)

 An American mechanical engineer and the first management consultant. He was born into a Quaker family, cleared the Harvard law entrance, but chose to become an apprentice machinist, working his way up from the shop floor.

identified massive inefficiency and constant friction between laborers and management

## Management Theory

**Time and Motion Studies** : stopwatches to strictly time every task -> Finds best way to do a task

**Differential Piece Rate System** : where workers were paid based on the number of pieces produced relative to the standard. This incentivized highly productive workers

**Selection, Training, and Ergonomics** : assessing workers' physical and mental aptitudes before assigning roles. He introduced systematic training

**Division of Labor and Standardization** : separated managerial duties from technical work

**Functional Foremanship:** Taylor replaced the traditional single supervisor with multiple specialized foremen
