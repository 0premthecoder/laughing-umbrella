# Historical evolution of Operation

|**Historical period**|**Dominant form of the function**|**Major conceptual developments**|
|---|---|---|
|Pre-industrial era|Craft production and guild systems|Small-batch production, skilled artisans, local supply and customized output.|
|Industrial Revolution|Factory production and mechanization|Division of labor, machinery, factory discipline, scale economies and production scheduling.|
|1900s-1930s|Scientific management and mass production|Standardization, time-and-motion study, assembly lines, work measurement and process control.|
|1940s-1960s|Operations research and quality control|Linear programming, queuing, inventory models, statistical quality control and wartime logistics methods.|
|1970s-1990s|Lean, J-I-T and TQM|Toyota Production System, Kanban, continuous improvement, waste elimination, quality circles and supplier integration.|
|2000s-2010s|Global supply chain and service operations|Global sourcing, outsourcing, ERP integration, supply chain risk, servitization and omnichannel operations.|
|2020s-present|AI-enabled operations|Digital twins, smart factories, autonomous planning, predictive maintenance, robotics, computer vision and AI supply chain control towers.|
