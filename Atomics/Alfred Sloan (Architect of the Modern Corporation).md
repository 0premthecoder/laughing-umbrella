# Alfred Sloan (Architect of the Modern Corporation)

American business executive, CEO, and Chairman of General Motors (GM). He authored the classic business text, _My Years with General Motors_.

## Management Theory

**M-Form (Multi-Divisional) Structure:** Sloan established a template for decentralized operations. GM's brands (Chevrolet, Pontiac, Oldsmobile, Buick, Cadillac) operated as semi-autonomous divisions.

**Product Differentiation Strategy:** He countered Henry Ford's single-product strategy ("buy any car as long as it is black")

**Management by Data:** Sloan institutionalized the use of financial metrics, specifically Return on Investment (ROI), and strict financial reporting.

**Professional Management:** Sloan finalized the transition from founder-led companies to modern corporations led by trained, professional managers working on behalf of shareholders.
