Here is the table from 

| **Theory**      | **Key Thinkers**           | **Core Focus**                   | **Business Applications**                  |
| --------------- | -------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Scientific Mgmt | Taylor, Gilbreth, Gantt    | Efficiency, task optimization    | SOPs, lean ops, assembly lines             |
| Administrative  | Fayol, Weber               | Structure, rules, Hierarchy      | Org charts, bureaucracy, HR policies       |
| Human Relations | Mayo, Maslow, McGregor     | People, motivation, social needs | Engagement, culture, coaching              |
| Quantitative    | Operations Research teams  | Mathematical decision models     | Supply chain optimization, ERP             |
| Systems Theory  | Bertalanffy, Katz & Kahn   | Holistic, interdependency        | Cross-functional teams, agile              |
| Contingency     | Lawrence & Lorsch, Fiedler | Situational fit                  | Adaptive leadership, context-specific mgmt |
