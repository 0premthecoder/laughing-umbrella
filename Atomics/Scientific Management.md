# Scientific Management

Given by Frederick Winslow Taylor with Gilberths * Henry Gantt

Apply Scientific analysis to tasks to maximize worker efficiency. Each job broken into measurable steps, and the 'one best way' identified through time and motion studies 

Key principles
Science, not rule of thumb
Scientific Selection & training of workers
Cooperation between management & labour
Division of work & responsibility

Some applications are 
Standardization
Incentive Pay
Time studies
