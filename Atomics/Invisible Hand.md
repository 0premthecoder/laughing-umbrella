# Invisible Hand

Core Idea
Individual pursuing private gain unintentionally promote the public good, as if guided by an invisible hand

Price Mechanism:
Supply and demand signals allocate resources more efficiently than any central planner could

Competition as Regulator:
Market Rivalry keeps Prices low and quality high No authority needed.

Policy Implication:
Govt Should Remove barrier to trade;
Free Market self-correct over time.

Management Implication:
Underpin Market based strategy; competitive advantage, Pricing Power and Industry analysis
