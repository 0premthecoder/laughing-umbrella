# Price Elasticity of Demand

A measure of how much the quantity demanded changes when the price changes
Sensitivity of the quantity demanded for the price

It is also called Elasticity of Demand, Demand Elasticity or even elasticity

**Elastic Demand** : Quantity demanded responds substantially to price changes

**Inelastic Demand** : Quantity Demanded responds only slightly to price changes
