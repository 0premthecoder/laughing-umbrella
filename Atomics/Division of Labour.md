# Division of Labour

By dividing the work among worker who specialize in a narrow Field of Activities can Impact highest Productivity Possible

Dexterity 
Repetition of a Single Task builds unmatched skill speed and precision

Time saving 
Eliminating task-switching cuts dead time between operation 

Machine Invention
Focused Workers more readily devise tools to erase their specific task

Management Implication
Basis for Scientific management, assembly line, and BPO/outsourcing logic.
