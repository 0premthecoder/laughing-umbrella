# Rewards and Challenges of Being a manager

## Rewards

• Creating a conducive environment for employees to do their best work.

• Opportunities for creative thinking and imagination.

• Helping others find meaning, fulfillment, and nurturing talent (coaching/mentoring).

• Working on a variety of problems.

• Motivating, stimulating, and inspiring others.

• Gaining recognition, status, and appropriate compensation.

• Leading and leaving a legacy.


## Challenges of Management

• Emotionally/Psychologically Demanding: Dealing with personal, professional, and team issues; managing a wide variety of personalities.

• Operational Burden: Mundane tasks like meetings, emails, and data allocation can outweigh strategic duties.

• Resource Constraints: High accountability for goals despite limited resources.

• Uncertainty/Chaos: Maintaining motivation through rapid changes and instability.

• Dependency: Success relies on the performance and delivery of others.

• Partial Control: Managing through influence rather than full authority; accountability without complete power.
