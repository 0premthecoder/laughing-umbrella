# Henry Fayol (Administrative Management Theory)

A mining engineer who spent his entire career at one company. Unlike Taylor's shop-floor view, Fayol developed his theories from a top-down, administrative perspective.

## Management Theory

- **Five Functions of Management:** He defined management through five enduring functions: Planning, Organizing, Commanding, Coordinating, and Controlling.

- **14 Principles of Management** [[Classical Theory]]

- **The Gang Plank (Bridge):** Recognizing the flaws of strictly vertical communication, Fayol proposed horizontal communication between peers at the same organizational level

- **Management as a Teachable Skill:** Fayol rejected the idea that management was an innate talent. By separating technical skills from managerial skills, he proved management could be taught
