# Characteristics of Marketing Myopia

**Product Obsession** - Focus only on existing product
**Ignoring customer needs** - Failure to understand evolving preferences
**Technological rigidity** - Resistance to innovation
**Short term Orientation** - Focus on immediate sales instead of long term value
**Market blindness** - Failure to notice environmental change
**Overconfidence** - Belief that current success will continue indefinitely
