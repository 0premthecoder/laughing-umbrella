# Five Function of Management

1. PLANNING: Setting goals, developing strategies, and outlining tasks and schedules to accomplish goals. It is the starting point of all management action. 
	- Planning Pitfalls
		- Paralysis by Analysis
		- Ignoring Environmental Challenges
		- Rigid Plans that don't adapt
		- Planning for appearance, not action

2. ORGANIZING: Arranging work to accomplish organizational goals; includes dividing work, assigning authority, and allocating resources.

3. STAFFING: Determining human-resource needs; recruiting, selecting, training, appraising, and retaining the right people.

4. LEADING: Motivating, directing, and influencing people to work hard to achieve goals.

5. CONTROLLING: Monitoring actual performance, comparing it to standards, and taking corrective action where needed. we use [[CSF]], [[KPI]]
