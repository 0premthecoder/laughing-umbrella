# Management = Art, Science & Craft

Science 
- Evidence based decision; data analytics; financial modelling
- Without: Disorganizing managing 

Art 
- Creative leaps; intuition; sensing opportunity
- without: dispirited managing

Craft
- Tactic knowledge; learning by doing; operational wisdom
- without: disconnected managing

Henry Mintzberg said management is combination of all
