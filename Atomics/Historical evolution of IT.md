# Historical evolution of IT

| **Historical period**          | **Dominant form of the function**              | **Major conceptual developments**                                                                                           |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Late 19th-early 20th centuries | Mechanized data processing                     | Punch cards, tabulators, payroll processing, census data and accounting mechanization.                                      |
| 1950s-1960s                    | Electronic data processing                     | Mainframes, batch processing, transaction records, payroll, billing and inventory control.                                  |
| 1970s-1980s                    | MIS and DSS                                    | Management information systems, decision support systems, databases, office automation and end-user computing.              |
| 1990s                          | Enterprise integration                         | ERP, client-server architecture, business process reengineering, enterprise data and integrated functional systems.         |
| 2000s                          | Internet and digital business                  | E-commerce, platforms, CRM, data warehouses, cybersecurity and IT outsourcing.                                              |
| 2010s                          | Cloud, mobile, analytics and DevOps            | Cloud infrastructure, agile development, APIs, big data, mobile apps, microservices and product management.                 |
| 2020s-present                  | AI platforms and agentic enterprise technology | Foundation models, enterprise AI platforms, MLOps, data governance, AI copilots, agentic workflows and zero-trust security. |
