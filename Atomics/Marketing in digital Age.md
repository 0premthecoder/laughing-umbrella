# Marketing in digital Age

From Push to pull marketing 
- Now rather than traditional ads
- the content is attractive and engages users

Data driven Marketing 
- Using Google analytics, Meta ads manager, sales force CRM software
- with this data every campaign can be measured with ROI in real time

Marketing Mix evolutions 
- from 4ps = Product, Price, Place, Promotion (introduced by E. Jerome McCarthy)
- extended to
- 7ps = People, Process, and Physical Evidence

Customers Journey Mapping
- Modern Marketing maps the full journey - awareness, consideration, purchase, retention, advocacy
- each stage requires different metric and strategy

Brand as Strategic asset
- Brand equity is a core balance-sheet consideration, even if accounting does not capture it. Apple brand value range between $500 billion to $1.3 trillion
