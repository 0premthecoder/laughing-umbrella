# Roles and Activities in Business Strategies

|**ROLES**|**KEY ACTIVITIES**|**STRATEGIC GOALS**|
|---|---|---|
|Chief Executive Officer (CEO)|Industry analysis and competitive intelligence|Build and sustain competitive advantage|
|Chief Strategy Officer (CSO)|Portfolio management: invest, grow, harvest, divest|Allocate capital to highest-return opportunities|
|Corporate Development / M&A Team|Mergers, acquisitions & strategic alliances|Create long-term value for all stakeholders|
|Strategy & Planning Analysts|Scenario planning and strategic risk management|Define and redefine the boundaries of the firm|
|Business Intelligence Lead|OKR (Objectives & Key Results) setting and cascading|Achieve strategic goals through aligned execution|
|Management Consultants (external)|Strategic communication to board, investors & employees|Position the organization for future industry structures|
