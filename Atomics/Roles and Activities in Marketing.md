# Roles and Activities in Marketing

|**ROLES**|**KEY ACTIVITIES**|**STRATEGIC GOALS**|
|---|---|---|
|Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)|Market segmentation, targeting & positioning (STP)|Build brand awareness and equity|
|Brand Manager|Product development & pricing strategy|Acquire and retain customers profitably|
|Digital Marketing Manager|Advertising, PR & brand management|Increase market share in target segments|
|Market Research Analyst|Digital marketing: SEO, SEM, social media|Drive revenue growth through demand creation|
|Product Marketing Manager|Customer relationship management (CRM)|Maximise Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)|
|Content & Social Media Lead|Sales funnel management & lead generation|Create differentiation vs competitors|
