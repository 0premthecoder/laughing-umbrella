# IT in Ai Era

Enterprise Al architecture: IT must integrate models, data pipelines, applications,
APIs, identity management, monitoring and security.

MLOps and LLMOps: Organizations require disciplined lifecycle management for
models, prompts, evaluation, deployment and drift monitoring.

Al agents: Enterprise software is moving from passive systems of record to action-
oriented systems that can summarize, recommend and execute tasks under
governance.

Cybersecurity escalation: Al strengthens threat detection but also increases
phishing, deepfake, prompt-injection and data-exfiltration risks.

IT operating model change: Central IT, product teams, data offices and business
functions increasingly co-own Al-enabled workflows.
