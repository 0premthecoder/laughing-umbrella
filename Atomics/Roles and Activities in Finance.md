# Roles and Activities in Finance

|**ROLES**|**KEY ACTIVITIES**|**STRATEGIC GOALS**|
|---|---|---|
|Chief Financial Officer (CFO)|Financial planning, budgeting & forecasting|Maximise shareholder value creation|
|Controller / Comptroller|Capital structure decisions (debt vs equity)|Maintain financial stability and liquidity|
|Treasurer|Cash flow & working capital management|Optimize capital allocation across business units|
|FP&A Lead|Mergers, acquisitions & due diligence|Ensure regulatory compliance and governance|
|Risk Manager|Financial reporting & regulatory compliance|Provide financial intelligence for strategic decisions|
|Investor Relations Manager|Risk identification, measurement & hedging|Manage investor relationships & market expectations|
