# Market Structures by Competition Level

| **Type**                     | **Characteristics**                                         | **Example**                      |
| ---------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **Perfect competition**      | Many sellers, identical products                            | Agricultural commodities         |
| **Monopoly**                 | One dominant seller                                         | Public utilities in some regions |
| **Oligopoly**                | Few large firms                                             | Global airline industry          |
| **Monopolistic competition** | Many differentiated sellers selling same product or similar | Restaurants                      |
