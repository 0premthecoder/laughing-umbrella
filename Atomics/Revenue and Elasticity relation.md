# Revenue and Elasticity relation
## The Rule for Making Money

- If your product is **inelastic** (like oil or medicine), **raise the price** to make more money.
    
- If your product is **elastic** (like games or movie tickets), **lower the price** to make more money.

so for inelastic demand no matter what happen demand remains the same
