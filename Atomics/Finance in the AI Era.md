# Finance in the AI Era

Autonomous close and continuous accounting: Ai and automation reduce manual reconciliation

Predictive FP&A: Finance teams use machine learning for demand forecasting, cash-flow prediction

Risk Intelligence: Ai improve fraud detection, credit scoring, anomaly detection, cyber-financial risk monitoring and regulatory surveillance

Generative Ai copilots: CFO teams increasingly use natural language interfaces to interrogate ledgers, draft commentary, explain variances and prepare management reports

Governance challenge: Finance AI requires auditable data lineage, model validation, segregation of duties, privacy controls and human review for material decision
