# Operation in Ai Era

Al planning systems: Demand sensing, inventory optimization, production
scheduling and transport routing are becoming increasingly algorithmic.

Digital twins: Firms model factories, supply chains and assets virtually to simulate
disruptions, maintenance and capacity decisions.

Predictive and prescriptive maintenance: Sensor data and machine learning
forecast equipment failures and recommend interventions.

Computer vision and robotics: Quality inspection, warehouse automation and
safety monitoring are increasingly supported by Al.

Al supply chain governance: The bottleneck is often not model accuracy alone but
data quality, process standardization, decision rights and human trust.
