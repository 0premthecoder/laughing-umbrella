# Stakeholder

Business should create value for all stakeholder affected by the organization

Stakeholder: [[Shareholder]], employees, customers, suppliers, communities, governments, and environment 

Stakeholder theory argue that
- companies must balance multiple interests
- not merely maximize shareholder wealth

|**Stakeholder**|**Interest**|
|---|---|
|**Shareholders**|Returns on investment|
|**Employees**|Salary, growth, security|
|**Customers**|Quality and value|
|**Suppliers**|Stable business relationships|
|**Government**|Taxes and compliance|
|**Society**|Ethical behaviour|
|**Environment**|Sustainability|
