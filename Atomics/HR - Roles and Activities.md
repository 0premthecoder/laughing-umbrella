# HR - Roles and Activities

| **ROLES**                         | **KEY ACTIVITIES**                            | **STRATEGIC GOALS**                               |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Chief People Officer (CPO / CHRO) | Workforce planning & talent acquisition       | Attract and retain the best talent                |
| Talent Acquisition Lead           | Onboarding, training & development programmed | Build organizational capability for future needs  |
| Learning & Development Manager    | Performance management & appraisal systems    | Create a high-performance culture                 |
| Compensation & Benefits Manager   | Compensation design & total rewards strategy  | Ensure fairness, compliance & employee well-being |
| HR Business Partner (HRBP)        | Employee engagement & retention initiatives   | Align human capital with business strategy        |
| DEI Lead                          | HR compliance, labor law & governance         | Develop the next generation of leadership         |
