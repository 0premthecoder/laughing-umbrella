# Why firm Exists

Without firm
- Every activity would require separate contracts
- constant bargaining would occur
- coordination would become inefficient

Firms reduces transaction costs through
- hierarchy
- managerial authority
- routine coordination
- stable employment relationships

### Insights
Market is not free to use

There are transactional costs, firms exist so they can organize economic activity more efficiently than markets

A firm expands until: Internal Coordination Cost = Market Transaction Costs

so Organization Grows shrink, outsource, or integrate depending on which arrangement minimizes transactional costs
