# Managing Ways Heroic vs Engaging

|**HEROIC MANAGEMENT:‘Managers are special people apart from others’**|**ENGAGING MANAGEMENT:‘Managers are important insofar as they help others be important.”**|
|---|---|
|❑ Strategy flows DOWN: bold, deliberate, top-down. Everyone else ‘implements.’|❑ An organization is an interacting NETWORK, not a vertical hierarchy.|
|❑ CEO at top = embodiment of the corporation.|❑ Strategies EMERGE from the network; small problems solved become big initiatives.|
|❑ **Risk:** Disconnects leadership from operational reality. CEO-centric organizations collapse when the CEO leaves.|❑ **Example:** Google’s 20% time - engineer exploration became Gmail, Maps, AdSense.|
