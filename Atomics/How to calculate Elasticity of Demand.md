# How to calculate Elasticity of Demand

Price Elasticity of Demand  = $$Percentage_Change_in_Quantity \over percentage_Change_in_Price$$
![[Pasted image 20260701170857.png|697]]

Goods for which the Price elasticity is greater than one have an elastic demand

Goods for which the Price Elasticity of demand is less than one have an inelastic demand

When its equal to one is Unit Elasticity

For a linear demand curve $Q = a - bP$, unit elasticity occurs at the midpoint of the price axis,
which is $a/(2b)$.
