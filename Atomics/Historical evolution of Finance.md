# Historical evolution of Finance

|**Historical period**|**Dominant form of the function**|**Major conceptual developments**|
|---|---|---|
|Ancient to medieval commerce|Bookkeeping, stewardship and merchant finance|Records of trade, credit, taxation, partnership accounts and risk sharing.|
|15th-19th centuries|Accounting and capital formation|Double-entry bookkeeping, joint-stock companies, stock exchanges, insurance and commercial banking.|
|Early 20th century|Financial control and budgeting|Budgetary control, cost accounting, managerial accounting and treasury administration.|
|1950s-1970s|Modern corporate finance|Discounted cash flow, portfolio theory, Modigliani-Miller propositions, CAPM, efficient market hypothesis.|
|180s-2000s|Value-based finance and risk management|Shareholder value, agency theory, derivatives, financial engineering, enterprise risk management and balanced performance systems.|
|2010s-present|Digital finance and AI-enabled finance|Fintech, cloud ERP, real-time dashboards, robotic process automation, predictive analytics and generative AI in finance workflows.|
