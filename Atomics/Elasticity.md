# Elasticity

Elasticity Measures the sensitivity of one economic variable to a change in another

It is a ratio of percentage in change in variables 

It shows the how much people (buyers and seller) respond to change in condition of market

It tells direction and size of the change 

Direction is determined by Negativity if it is negativity that means Price and Demand are Inversely Proportional

Size is determined by Strength of no

Goods for which the Price elasticity is greater than one have an elastic demand

Goods for which the Price Elasticity of demand is less than one have an inelastic demand
