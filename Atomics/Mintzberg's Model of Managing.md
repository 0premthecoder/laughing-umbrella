# Mintzberg's Model of Managing

1. Setting goals and laying out plans: creating direction and alignment
2. Motivating and aligning effort: harnessing people's energy towards goals
3. Coordinating and controlling activities: ensuring the parts work as a whole
4. Accumulating and allocating resources: decisions about money, people and time
5. Acquiring and applying knowledge: learning, sense-making, and decision-making
6. Building and nurturing relationships: internal networks and external constituencies 
7. Identifying and developing talent: the manager as a coach and talent scout
8. Balancing demands of outside constituencies: stakeholder management
