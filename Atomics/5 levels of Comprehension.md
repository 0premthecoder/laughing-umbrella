# 5 levels of Comprehension

|**Levels**|**Labels**|**Explanations**|**Examples**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**1**|Literal Understanding|Understanding explicit information|Revenue increased by 10%|
|**2**|Interpretive Understanding|Understanding implications|Revenue increased because:<br><br>  <br><br>• Market share increased<br><br>  <br><br>• New customers were acquired|
|**3**|Analytical Understanding|Understanding relationships|Revenue increased despite industry decline. Possible competitive advantage exists.|
|**4**|Strategic Understanding|Understanding broader significance|Revenue growth indicates a structural shift in customer preferences.|
|**5**|Systems Understanding|Understanding interactions among multiple variables|Revenue growth affects:<br><br>  <br><br>• Capacity<br><br>  <br><br>• Hiring<br><br>  <br><br>• Supply chain<br><br>  <br><br>• Cash flow<br><br>  <br><br>• Investor expectations|
