# What is an Industry

A industry is group of [[What is a Firm]] producing similarly or closing related products or services
Firms within an industry compete with one another


| Characteristics   | Explaination                              |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Similar Output    | Firms Producing related products/services |
| Competition       | Firms Compete for Customer                |
| Shared technology | Often use similar technology              |
| Similar Customers | Serve Related Market needs                |
| Common Regulation | Operate under similar rules               |
