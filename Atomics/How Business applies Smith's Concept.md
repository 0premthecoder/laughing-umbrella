# How Business applies Smith's Concept

|**Business Domain**|**Smith's Concept**|**Modern Business Application**|
|---|---|---|
|**Operations Management**|Division of Labour|Assembly lines, lean manufacturing, task decomposition, BPO, shared services centres.|
|**Competitive Strategy**|Invisible Hand / Market Competition|Porter's Five Forces, competitive dynamics, pricing strategy, market entry decisions.|
|**HRM / Incentives**|Self-Interest as Motivator|Performance-linked pay, commission structures, agency theory, stock options.|
|**International Business**|Comparative Advantage / Free Trade|Global supply chains, trade policy advocacy, offshoring location decisions.|
|**Corporate Finance**|Capital Theory (Fixed vs. Circulating)|Capital allocation, CapEx vs. OpEx decisions, working capital management.|
|**Public Policy & Regulation**|Limited Government / Free Markets|Deregulation, privatisation, anti-monopoly advocacy, WTO trade rules.|
|**Entrepreneurship**|Market Opportunity & Price Signals|Spotting arbitrage opportunities; market gaps signalled by price differentials.|
|**Organisational Design**|Functional Specialisation|Departmentalisation, centres of excellence, knowledge management.|
