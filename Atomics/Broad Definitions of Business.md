# Broad Definitions of Business

| **Feature**     | **Product-oriented**           | **Market-oriented**               |
| --------------- | ------------------------------ | --------------------------------- |
| **Focus**       | The product or service offered | The customer need being fulfilled |
| **Perspective** | Inward-looking                 | Outward-looking                   |
| **Orientation** | Production-centric             | Customer-centric                  |

### Examples of Business Definitions

|**Company**|**Product-oriented definition**|**Market-oriented definition**|
|---|---|---|
|**Amazon**|We sell books, toys, consumer electronics and other products.|We make the Internet buying experience fast, easy, enjoyable.|
|**3M**|We sell adhesives, sealants and fillers (Post-It Notes, Scotch Tape...).|We solve people's problems by putting innovation to work for them.|
|**Tata Motors**|We manufacture vehicles.|We provide safe, affordable personal & commercial mobility solutions.|
|**HDFC Bank**|We provide banking services.|We help customers achieve their financial aspirations at every life stage.|
|**Amul**|We produce dairy products.|We nourish India with quality food at prices every family can afford.|
