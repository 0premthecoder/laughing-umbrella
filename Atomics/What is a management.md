# What is a management


In People Centric
- Management is fundamentally about people
- Follet's Insight: "True leadership isn't about enforcing power over others, but increasing the sense of power within those led"

In Context-sensitive
- Management is not a formula
- It adapts to industry, culture, crises and growth stages
	- A manager succeeded in startup may fail in bureaucratic way

SO, Management 
is [[Process]] of Coordinating work activities so that they are completed efficiently and effectively with and through other people
