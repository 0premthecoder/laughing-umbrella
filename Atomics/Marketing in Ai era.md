# Marketing in Ai era

Ai-enabled personalization
- Predictive customer behavior
- to tailor offers and recommendation to individual customer

Content generation at scale
- GenAI supports first drafts of copy, product descriptions, campaign concepts etc
- while brand governance and human review remain essential

Customer journey orchestration
- Ai links segmentation, next best action, campaign timing, channel choice, churn prediction and service recovery across touchpoints

Marketing analytics and experimentation
- AI improves attributes, media mix modelling, A/B split testing, synthetic audience simulation
- pricing experiments and real time campaign optimization

Conversational Commerce 
- Chatbots, voice assistants and agentic customer interfaces convert marketing from one-way communication to interactive advisory and transaction support 

Privacy, trust and brand safety
- The Ai-era must manage consent, data minimization, bias, deepfake risk, intellectual property, transparency and reputational risk

Operating Model Change
- Marketing teams are becoming more integrated with data science, product, sales, legal, IT and customer success

shifting from campaign execution to growth system change
