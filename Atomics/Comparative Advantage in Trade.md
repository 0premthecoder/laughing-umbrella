# Comparative Advantage in Trade

Gain from trade arises with [[Comparative Advantage]] 

When each country produces goods in which it has comparative advantage it leads to a bigger economic pie overall 

Same could be applied for individual producer

but For trade between two parties to make both better off, the trading price Lies Strictly Between two parties' [[opportunity cost]]

Seller < Cost < Buyer
