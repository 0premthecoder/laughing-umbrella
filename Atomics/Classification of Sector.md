# Classification of Sector

Classification Based on Economics

|**Sector**|**Definition**|**Activities**|**Examples**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Primary**|Extracts natural resources directly from nature.|Agriculture, Fishing, Mining, Forestry|Oil and Gas: ONGC; Mining: Coal India|
|**Secondary**|Converts raw materials into finished goods.|Manufacturing, Construction, Industrial production|Automobile: BMW; Cement: Ultra Tech|
|**Tertiary**|Provides services rather than physical products.|Banking, Education, Healthcare, Retail, IT services|IT Services: Infosys; Banking: ICICI Bank|
|**Quaternary**|Knowledge-based and information-intensive services.|Research, Artificial intelligence, Analytics, Software development, Consulting|Google, Open AI, Zoho|
|**Quinary**|Highest-level decision-making and advanced knowledge activities.|Government leadership, Scientific leadership, Top corporate management, Policy institutions|McKinsey & Company, World Bank|
